Heidi Klum Just Posted a Rare Photo of All Four of Her Kids Together

When you're a supermodel and TV host, taking your kids to work with you looks a little different than it does for the average parent. This week, Heidi Klum posted a photo of all four of her kids on Instagram with the caption, "Today it's …… bring ALL your kids to work day." The group was in a van together headed to the set of Germany's Next Topmodel, which Klum hosts.

Klum often posts photos and videos of herself having fun with her kids, but it's more rare for her to share pics that show all of them together. Klum shares her four children—16-year-old Leni, 15-year-old Henry, 14-year-old Johan, and 11-year-old Lou—with ex-husband Seal. Leni's biological father is Italian businessman Flavio Briatore, but she was later adopted by Seal.
