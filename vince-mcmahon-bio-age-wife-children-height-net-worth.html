Vince Mcmahon Bio, Age, Wife, Children, Height, Net Worth

Vince McMahon was born Vincent Kennedy McMahon.

Vince McMahon is an American business executive, media practitioner and professional wrestling promoter and performer. 

Vince has from 1982 to 2022 served on the Board of the WWE as Chairman and Chief Executive Officer. 

Table of Contents

Vince McMahon Age: How Old Is McMahon?

Vince McMahon was born on August 24, 1945. He was born in Pinehurst, North Carolina, United States. 

Vince McMahon Wife: Who Is Linda McMahon?

Vince McMahon is married to Linda McMahon. The couple were joined together as husband and wife in 1966 and have remained married to date. Linda is an American political executive and former wrestler. She served as an administrator of the Small Business Administration during the Trump administration. Linda was born on October 4, 1948. 

Vince McMahon Children: Meet  Stephanie McMahon and Shane McMahon

Vince McMahon and his wife Linda have two children. They are  Stephanie McMahon and Shane McMahon. Stephanie is a businesswoman and a retired wrestler. She is a fourth-generation wrestling promoter. Stephanie in recent times has had to resign from WWE after her father announced his return. Shane is also a businessman and a retired wrestler. He was born on January 15, 1970, and is the founder of ideanomics.
