AEW Rampage Slightly Down, Demo Rating Rises On 1/26

AEW Rampage viewership dipped on January 26.

Wrestlenomics reports that 382,000 viewers tuned in on Friday. This is slightly down from last week, when 390,000 viewers watched the show.

In the 18 to 49 demographic, Rampage scored a 0.13 rating. This number is slightly up from last week (0.12 rating in the same demographic).
