Inside Bobby Brazier's love life: from Strictly champ to EastEnders co-star

The EastEnders star will be competing in Soccer Aid

    Bobby Brazier is heading onto the pitch for this year's Soccer Aid and the actor will certainly be one to watch as he proved his sportiness when he finished as a runner-up on last year's Strictly Come Dancing.

    The 21-year-old actor, is set to play on the England team and will no doubt be hoping to turn around their fortune following their losses in the previous five years. But will the EastEnders star have a special someone cheering him on during the match? Find out all we know about the model, including his dating life...

    Bobby's life

    Bobby Brazier is a 21-year-old actor from Harlow. His parents are TV presenter Jeff Brazier and reality star Jade Goody, who passed away from cervical cancer in 2009.

    Bobby began his career as a model after being scouted by agent Cesar Perin when he was just 16. He made his runway debut in 2020, modelling for Dolce & Gabbana at Milan Fashion Week, before going on to appear at London Fashion Week and Paris Fashion Week.

    In September 2022, he made his first appearance, playing Freddie Slater, the son of Little Mo Mitchell. The following year, he won the gong for Rising Star at the 28th National Television Awards for his performance. Last year, he joined the cast of and he and professional partner Dianne Buswell managed to make it to the final, losing out to Ellie Leach and Vito Coppola. 

    Dating history

    Bobby is currently believed to be single. The actor was previously in a relationship with influencer and model Liberty Love. Bobby, who is thought to have dated Liberty for around 18 months, confirmed their split in a TikTok video released ahead of his debut. 

    In the video, Bobby was stopped on the street in London by a content creator who asked when the last time he "had a girl round to his place". Bobby replied: "Probably my ex-girlfriend months ago. I'm a nice person." When asked why the couple had gone their separate ways, Bobby responded: "It didn't match my priorities."

    The star has been linked to numerous ladies however, including his co-star Ellie Dadd, who joined the soap at a similar time to him.

    Plenty of romance rumours swirled around the actor during his time on and he even referenced a partner when he explained: "I woke up one morning doing this [arm gestures] I woke myself up doing that, crazy!  And then the week before, the person that I shared a bed with, I turned and said: 'This is the American Smooth'."

    One of the women Bobby was linked to was dance partner Dianne Buswell, even though she is in a relationship with Joe Sugg. Following what appeared to be a near-kiss following their Argentine Tango, the dancer clarified on Good Morning Britain: "There's nothing untoward going on here, it's just the dancing."

    Bobby was also linked with champion Ellie Leach, with the pair reportedly growing close while on the Strictly Live Tour.  Ellie appeared to 'confirm' the relationship when she went 'Instagram official', posting a charming black and white selfie with the star as they posed cheek to cheek. However, the couple reportedly went their separate ways following the Live Tour.

    Bobby has since been linked with another castmate, Jazzy Phoenix, however, the pair have not commented on the possibility of a relationship.
