Jerry Hughes Memorial Service

A Celebration of Life for Jerry Hughes will be held at 2 p.m. on Thursday, Jan. 26, in the Elliott Student Union Ballrooms on the campus of UCM.

Parking will be available in the visitor lot south of the Elliott Student Union and the large faculty/staff/student lot south of the Wood building. Those who wish to view the service via livestream may do so at www.ucmo.edu/live-event. The link will be active by 1:30 p.m. on Thursday.

The family requests that memorial contributions be made in honor of Jerry to the University of Central Missouri Alumni Foundation directed to Mule Train or the Eleanor G. Hughes and Carol Dyetta Hughes Literacy Program.

Jerry Hughes, who dedicated more than 40 years to laying the foundation for successes that included regional and national athletics championships, improved sports playing facilities, and the recruitment of thousands of outstanding student-athletes at the University of Central Missouri, passed away late Saturday, Jan. 21, after a brief illness.

Read more about Jerry and his dedication and service to UCM here: https://bit.ly/3HoPA8Y
