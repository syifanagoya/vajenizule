Martin Wallström is a Swedish actor and voiceover artist who is best known for having been cast as Tyrell Wellick in the drama thriller television series Mr. Robot. He has also played a variety of roles in other shows and movies including Ashes in the Snow, Beyond the Border, Easy Money III: Life Deluxe, Nirbashito, Upp till kamp, Jeff And Some Aliens, Wallander, and Oskyldigt dömd.

    Martin Wallström Facts

  • He started acting professionally during his teen years.
  • Alongside Bill Skarsgård and Cecilia Forss, he co-starred as Sam in the 2010 comedy film, Simple Simon (Swedish: I rymden finns inga känslor), which was shortlisted for that year’s Academy Award for “Best Foreign Language Film”.
  • From 2015 and 2019, Martin Wallström appeared as Tyrell Wellick, originally the ambitious Senior Vice President of Technology at E Corp and later part of fsociety, in the American techno drama thriller television series, Mr. Robot.
  • He was cast as Nikolai Kretzsky in the Lithuanian-American World War II drama film, Ashes in the Snow, in 2018.
  • He is fluent in Swedish and English.
  • Martin has narrated a number of audiobooks in Swedish.
